Temp-O-Matic

The Temp-O-Matic is a programmable thermometer that allows the user to record the temperature of food cooling at customizable increments. Many restaurants and food businesses rely on certain proceedures to cool food quickly to avoid bacterial growth. This device takes a temperature reading every interval and records it to internal memory. It beeps and sends a notification to a computer via email or text message if the food is not on track to cool safely so other methods can be implemented.
